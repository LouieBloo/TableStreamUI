Table Stream

Welcome to Table Stream! A platform that lets you connect, share your webcam, and enjoy games like Magic: The Gathering with friends online.

Start Playing

How It Works

Table Stream allows you to join or create games for different formats like Commander, Standard, Modern, and more. Stream your table using your webcam and microphone to play with real cards.

Our platform will soon support other popular card games like Pokémon and Yu-Gi-Oh! Expand your gaming experience and connect with players worldwide.

Game Features

  • 2-4 player games
  • Search and share cards in-game
  • Real-time audio and video streaming
  • Spectator support
  • Password protected games
  • Game state tracking such as:
    • Life totals
    • Commander Damages
    • Energy
    • Poison
    • Monarch
    • Turn Timers
    • Cities Blessing

Planned Features

  • Yu-Gi-Oh! support
  • Pokémon support
  • Public server list
  • Click to auto search for cards
  • Emoticons and sound boards
  • Much more!

No Account Needed

Table Stream is completely free to use. No account or email registration is required. Just jump in and start playing!
